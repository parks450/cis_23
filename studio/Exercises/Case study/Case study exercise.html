Jiyeon's Website

Archive for Last Day of Highschool


The Project 2 "Last day of school" is a digital archive of only the last day of school

that Jiyeon collected from the internet by year.

The videotape imagery exemplifies the old-school mood that she's going for,

and clicking on each videotape plays a clip based on the name of the video.

The collection is organized from the 1960s to 2010, and pressing a year brings up

an interaction that shows the videotapes included in that year.
